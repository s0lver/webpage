Hello, welcome to this website!

I am a PhD student at LTI Cinvestav Tamaulipas (2014 - 2017)



Prior to starting my PhD, I got my Master Degree in Computer Sciences also at Cinvestav from 2010 to 2012.

A few years before that, I got a title as a Computing Systems Engineer at the ITCV.

Before thinking on taking school and knowledge so serious, I was a happy kid living in my hometown: San Fernando Tamaulipas.

I like to solve stuff, software development and being in contact with nature (I mean mountains and forests, not killing lions and so on xD).
